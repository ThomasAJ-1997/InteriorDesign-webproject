What our Full Interior Design Service offer?

+

Our full service interior design involves large scale and more complex projects for whole homes or multiple room projects where extensive remodelling works are required. These full service projects require detailed technical skills, a lot of person to person contact and co-ordination throughout the stage of the project, visiting our branches and associated manufacturing oversight, and the ongoing professional input from our experienced team.

How much do you charge for your design services

+

Our design fees for our full interior design services are based upon a percentage over the construction construction costs, but costs typically sit around 10%. Once an accurate scope of work is established a more detailed fee proposal can be prepared for you.

Is furniture, artwork and accessories included in the design fee?

+

No, our design fess are for the design plan and don't include additional costs of furnishings, art and accessories. Here at Sage & Hollows, the design will provide you with the complete Procurement, administration and handing service for your design. This service is provided on a whole scheme basis.
