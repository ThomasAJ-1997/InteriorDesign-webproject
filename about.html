About Us

Founded and run by London-based interior designer Jessica Rose. We've been designing and installing professional interiors for businesses, hotels and show rooms for 20+ years.

Jessica Rose, founder & Creative Director

Jessica Rose, Creative director of Sage & hollows founed her interior design company in 2002 and it a mulitple award winning interior designer with a extensive Portfolio supporting clients, businesses and the private sector. Jessica is recognised within her field with a SBID (Society of British and Internation Interior Design) accreditation.

Sage & Hollows so far wouldn't be in the limelight without Jessica's undeniably determination, design perspective and willingess for everybody around her to succeed. Her approach to design goes further than aesthectics but create emotional benefits of a space and how a space can improve the way you live within it, that is the primary goal Jessica strives for.

Jessica and her studio team at Sage & Hollows take the important time needed to truly understand all clients, and take into account their personalities, lifestyle and incorporate them into design ideas. Getting to know clients is top piority for the studio team, we all collectively believe that the success of any interior design business is how the space transforms a clients ideas into reality that bring a positive direct impact on their emotional wellbeing and ours.

Jessica has a strong link with Yorkshire and enjoys spending time in the area she was born in. Our home office is based in London, but our presence in Yorkshire allows her to take on projects in the heart of London and her beloved birthplace in Yorkshire.

Jessica is married, a mother of three children and a loved for Yorksire tea. Often accompanied by her two dogs Teddy and Marmaduke who also spends much of her time as an extended family and part of the studio.

As a collective team we're are devoted to Jessica's profound leadership style and motivation to succeed by leading a collaborative approach in our three studios across the UK, ensuring that each design is an experience at all stages of the process.

Our teams passions and aptitude for design has allowed us to create designs for high profile people and exceptional brand recognition. Our Portfolio of projects has allowed us to create schemes and embrace the delicate balance to design timeless luxury.

Our studio team acts as a family. We have attained experience collaborating for some of the most illustrious design-houses within the UK. Thanks to our wonderful clients and business we've worked with over the years, we're a well-informed studio encompassing a collection of skills and knowledge within the interior design sector.

I couldn't ask for a better team who share the same values and principles I do. I hope to work with them for many years. - Jessica Rose
