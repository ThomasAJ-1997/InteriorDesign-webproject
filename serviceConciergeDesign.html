Concierge Design Service

A personalised consulting service without the need for a full design, supporting clients with a one to one, perfectly tailored level of support - meeting you exactly where you need it most.

Concierge Interior Design Service

Our Design concierge service supports clients with an extensive range of luxury interior requests such as commissions for private artworks, bespoke carpets, custom cabinetry and styling services. We understand that our industry knowledge and expertise of luxury finishes alongside our established relationships with the finest artisans and suppliers allows us to offer a highly personalised concierge service. Perhaps you are undertaking your own project and would just like some expert help and advice with how to achieve your desired look. Our Design Concierge Service is available by the hour and can be conducted in person or remotely via video call.
