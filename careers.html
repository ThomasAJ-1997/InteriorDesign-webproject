Careers

At Sage & Hollows Interior Design we don't view our work as simply a 9-5 structure. To us it's a mission. A career where you are part of something bigger with a diverse and talented team who are passionate about each other and striving for to build their personal and professional development.

Sage & Hollows isn't just 'an interior design studio' - our amazing hard working team are worth more than that. We create experiences and enhance the lifestyles of our clients, thinking past the notion that design is about aesthetics only.

We embrace everybody's individuality within a relaxed, collaborative working environment and our growing team are dedicated, and loyal not only to their role in the business or duties but to each other as a unified and loving space.
