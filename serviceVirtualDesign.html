Virtual Design Service

Our approachable virtual design service is a personalised and bespoke design experience which is streamlined and crafted to meet the needs of our clients who are seeking a phased or small project.

Virtual Interior Design Service

Not all projects require a highly detailed and face to face service, our Virtual Interior Design Service is a tailored version of our Full Service Interior Design delivering an alternative approach to clients who are seeking a quicker or less complex design journey. This process embodies all of the quality and creativity of our Full Interior Design Service, organised in a streamlined and efficient manner, removing the need for extensive on-site visits, face to face consultations and revisions which allows us to offer a competitive fixed price fee according to room type and size.

Providing a more cost effective luxury design solution which is also particularly suited to remote projects or those seeking to make changes at their own pace from the comfort of their own home.

F&Q

Can I have a partial room designed?

+

Our Virtual Interior Design is for full rooms or spaces only, this ensures that the unique design that we create for you aligns with our design quality and perspective. This also allows us to deliver our best possible work for you, providing you with the greatest return on your investment by giving you a full transformation and cohesive look.

How does my contractor wok with the design

+

All questions specific to the contractor are best handled directly with them, utilising their professional expertise. Our design plan will capture descriptive details and the design intent, however we do not directly offer contractor services. Our priority is to offer you a clear design vision for you. Any drawings will be based upon the measurements that you have provided to us. Whilst we aim to ensure accuracy as far as possible on these drawings it is important to note that as we have not physically been to site the measurements are indicative only and we recommend that you get a site survey from all installers prior to them quoting and undertaking their works.

How long does a virtual design take?

+

Each project is unique with many factors affecting the timeline of deliverables. We strive to deliver our design plans within 14-16 weeks. The implementation of the design on-site will be in addition to this schedule and will be subject to the scope of works and the availability of any third party contractors. The delivery of a full package of furniture, artwork and accessories may take anything between 4-16 weeks from ordering.
