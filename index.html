About Us

Founded and run by London-based interior designer Jessica Rose. We've been designing and installing professional interiors for businesses, hotels and show rooms for 20+ years.

Today, we are a big family of qualified interior designers and managers with stylists, furnishers, framers and fitters. We cover the whole of the UK from our base home in the London Borough of Islington. We focus on representing forward thinking, creatve, mordern and multi-talented design to be the best business possible for our clients.
